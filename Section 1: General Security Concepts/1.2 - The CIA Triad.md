Integrity ensures that the data received is exactly what was sent â€” untouched and unmodified.

### Methods to ensure integrity:
- **Hashing**  
  - Sender calculates a hash and sends it with the data.  
  - Receiver hashes the data again; if both match, the data is unchanged.

- **Digital signatures**  
  - Uses asymmetric encryption + hashing.  
  - Confirms both data integrity *and* the identity of the sender.

- **Certificates**  
  - Validate identities of users or devices during communication.

- **Nonrepudiation**  
  - The sender cannot deny sending the data because its integrity and origin are proven.
